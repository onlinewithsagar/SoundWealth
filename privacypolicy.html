Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

At SoundWealth, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our expense tracking service.

1 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide you with our expense tracking service:

  • Account Information: Your email address, name, and password when you create an account
  • Financial Data: Expense amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates that you enter
  • Usage Information: How you interact with our website to help us improve our service
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device and browser for security purposes

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain your expense tracking account
  • To process and store your expense data securely
  • To send you important account notifications and updates
  • To improve our website features and user experience
  • To provide customer support when you need help
  • To detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access

3 Data Security

We take strong measures to protect your personal and financial information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted both when stored and when transmitted
  • Secure Servers: Our servers are protected with industry-standard security measures
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access user data, and only when necessary
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update our security systems to protect against new threats
  • Monitoring: We continuously monitor for suspicious activity and unauthorized access

4 Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data only in these situations:

  • Analytics Services: Anonymous usage data to help us understand how our service is used
  • Payment Processors: If you make payments, we share necessary information with secure payment providers
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our users' safety
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our service (they cannot use your data for other purposes)

We never share your actual expense data or financial details with third parties for marketing purposes.

5 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all your personal data in your account settings
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: You can download a copy of all your expense data
  • Opt-out: You can unsubscribe from non-essential emails

To exercise these rights, simply log into your account or contact us using the information below.

6 Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete this information.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • We will notify you by email if the changes significantly affect how we handle your data
  • We will post a notice on our website for 30 days after any major changes

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
