üöÄ Getting Started

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, enter your email address and create a secure password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account. Once verified, you can start tracking your expenses immediately.

After logging in, click the large "Add Expense" button on your dashboard. Enter the amount you spent, select a category (like Food, Transportation, etc.), add a brief description, and choose the date. Click "Save" and your expense will be recorded!

We provide common categories like Food & Dining, Transportation, Shopping, Entertainment, Bills & Utilities, Healthcare, and Travel. You can also create custom categories that fit your specific needs. Go to Settings > Categories to manage them.

Your dashboard shows your monthly spending summary, recent expenses, budget progress, spending trends, and quick action buttons. It's designed to give you an instant overview of your financial activity at a glance.

üë§ Account Management

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a reset link. Check your email (including spam folder) and follow the instructions to create a new password.

Go to Settings > Profile, click "Change Email," enter your new email address, and confirm with your current password. You'll need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.

We're sorry to see you go! Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm your decision and enter your password. Remember to export your data first if you want to keep it, as deletion is permanent after 30 days.

Yes! Go to Settings > Notifications to customize your email preferences. You can choose to receive budget alerts, monthly summaries, security notifications, and feature updates. You can turn any of these on or off.

Your data security is our top priority. We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption, secure servers, regular security audits, and strict access controls. Your data is encrypted both when stored and transmitted. We never store sensitive information like bank account numbers or credit card details.

üîß Troubleshooting

First, double-check your email and password for typos. Try using the "Forgot Password" feature. Clear your browser cache and cookies, or try a different browser or incognito mode. If you're still having trouble, contact our support team.

Check your internet connection first. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If you have many expenses (1000+), try filtering to smaller date ranges. Disable browser extensions that might interfere. Contact us if the problem persists.

Make sure you have expenses in the selected date range. Check your browser's download settings and popup blockers. Try a smaller date range if you have lots of data. Disable browser extensions that might block downloads. Contact support if it still doesn't work.

SoundWealth works best on modern browsers: Chrome (version 90+), Firefox (version 88+), Safari (version 14+), and Edge (version 90+). We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience and security.

Take a screenshot of the error message and note what you were doing when it occurred. Try refreshing the page first. If the error persists, contact our support team with the screenshot and steps to reproduce the issue. We'll investigate and fix it quickly!
